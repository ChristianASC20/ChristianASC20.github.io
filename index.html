It's uses include:

  1. Combo starting
  2. Combo extending
  3. Anti-airing
  4. Stuffing out approaces
  5. Confirming into kills

On top of all of this it begins tech chasing with the sour spot at really high %. It dosen't come up often, but it exist

It also tech chases through mid %, along with roy's high kock back leading to very earlty kills
